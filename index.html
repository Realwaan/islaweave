Artisan Spotlight

Nanay Elena Villamor

"Every thread I weave carries a prayer for the one who will hold it. This is not just craft — it is love made visible."

A master weaver from Argao, Cebu, Nanay Elena has been practicing the art of hablon weaving for over 40 years. Her intricate patterns draw from the natural landscapes of the Central Visayas — the waves of the sea, the terraces of the hills, and the geometry of traditional architecture.
